INTRODUCTION

In the midst of the mundane and the procrastination-laden days of my final exams in 2022, I stumbled upon the inception of a story born out of sheer boredom. It began as a whimsical distraction, but as the exam chaos subsided, so did my initial efforts, leaving only the fledgling first two parts in its wake.

Yet, as the calendar flipped to April 2023, the embers of creativity reignited, breathing life into what I now consider the crux of the narrative—Part 3. This digital rebirth not only marks the continuation of Yuta Tokito's journey but also serves as a testament to my evolving drawing skills and storytelling prowess, a metamorphosis documented in the "Draft Story."

This digital adaptation is more than just pixels on a screen; it's an invitation for everyone to partake in the evolving tapestry of this tale. A fusion of influences from beloved animanga like Attack on Titan, Bleach, Jujutsu Kaisen, Jojo's Bizarre Adventure, and more, the story weaves these inspirations into a unique fabric that I hope resonates with readers.

Centered around our protagonist, Yuta Tokito (formerly known as "Kujo Jotaro" in the draft), the narrative embarks on a journey of revenge and redemption. Yuta's quest to avenge his fallen family members unfolds against the backdrop of a sinister corporation, the nucleus of the antagonist's organization. Along the way, Yuta finds companions whose camaraderie and support become pivotal in his pursuit of justice.

As the story unfolds digitally, it invites you to join Yuta Tokito on his odyssey—a tale born of procrastination, inspired by animanga, and crafted with the hope that its essence resonates with the hearts of those who embark on this adventure.
